Day 42: Gooey Morph

Working with Contrast and Blur

I stumpled upon this great blog article by Rohn, decided to give it a shot. Instead of injecting the blur and contrast as strings in the style properties, Framer already accepts constrast and blur as direct property that can be adjusted outside of the style property. I gave it a shot, it worked. Swipe left to view effect. This is demo only works in Safari.
