Day 51: Wine Cart Rebound

Nested Loops, String to Integer, Animations, States, and Drag

I saw Anadea Inc's great prototpye and thought of a few ways to improve its UX. In my prototype the swipe gesture can be discovered via the add button. The interaction took way longer than I expected to build, nevertheless it was quite a very rewarding experience. I've learned a lot about how to use nested loops, parse string to integer and updating counts. Interact with the third wine to view the effects.
