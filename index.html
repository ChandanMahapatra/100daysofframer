Day 71: Pinterest-Inspired Zoom Effect

Utils.modulate(), and states

Seeing Joshua Tucker's awesomely annotated prototype, I decided to enhance the user experience of this toggle. For people like mom, who are not used to these mobile design pattern, bad copy writing never makes sense. "So it says 'hide my feed from this contact', which way should I push the toggle button? Does the green side mean show or hide?" One time she called and asked. The copy should never ask "not" question. Users should not have to think about doulbe-negatives.
